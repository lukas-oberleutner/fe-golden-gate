Slitky

A gold bar is a solid piece of pure gold that is cast in a rectangular or other shape and is used primarily as a form of investment. Gold bars come in a variety of sizes, with the most common sizes being 1 ounce, 10 ounces, and 1 kilogram. They are made by melting high-purity gold bullion and casting it into molds. Gold has been a symbol of wealth and prosperity for centuries, and owning gold bars is still a popular way to invest in this precious metal.
